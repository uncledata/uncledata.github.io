About Me

I'm a data professional with a passion for continuous learning and creating impactful solutions. My journey has taken me from Data Analyst to Data Engineer, with experiences in team leadership and individual contributor roles. What drives me is the desire to provide value, whether it's for the company I work for or the people I work with.

As an introvert who goes out of his way to help others, I find joy in sharing knowledge and empowering people. I believe in giving back to the community what I didn't have when starting my career in data. This motivation led me to create 'Uncle Data,' a platform where I share insights and experiences through writing and podcasting.

I thrive on tinkering with new technologies, always exploring how they can fit into various scenarios in my work environment. My approach to problem-solving involves guiding others towards the best solution through healthy discussions, rather than forcing opinions.

Whether it's leading a team, diving deep into complex technical challenges, or mentoring others, I'm always ready to adapt my role to where I can create the most value. My goal is to make a meaningful impact in the world of data engineering while helping others grow alongside me.
